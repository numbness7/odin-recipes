Smoothie

Description

This here is a healthy protein rich vegan smoothie that you can have for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen strawberries.
  • 1 cup frozen bananas.
  • 1/2 an apple (freezing optional).
  • 2 tablespoons peanut butter.
  • 2 cups soy milk.

Steps

  1. Chuck everything into a blender.
  2. Turn blender on to highest setting.
  3. Pour smoothie into cup.
