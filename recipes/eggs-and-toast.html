Eggs and Toast

Description

The above is eggs and toast. It's a protein rich breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs.
  • 2 slices of bread.
  • Some butter.

Steps

  1. Turn on a burner on an oven to medium.
  2. Put pan on top of burner.
  3. Put both slices of bread into toaster and toast them for three minutes.
  4. Crack open each of the eggs spilling their contents onto the pan.
  5. Let eggs cook for five minutes.
  6. Spread butter on toasted bread slices when they're cool enough to handle after putting them on a plate.
  7. When eggs are done cooking remove them from pan by putting them on a plate.
  8. Turn off the burner.
  9. Remove pan from burner.
  10. Serve breakfast.
