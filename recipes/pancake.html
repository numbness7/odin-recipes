Pancake

Description

This here is a healthy protein rich vegan pancake that you can have for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • Some vegan butter.
  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats.
  • 1 banana.
  • 1 cup vegan milk (soy milk).

Steps

  1. Turn on burner to medium.
  2. Put pan on burner.
  3. Put some vegan butter onto pan.
  4. Put banana, oats, and vegan milk into blender in the order stated.
  5. Turn blender on to highest setting.
  6. When done blending pour blender contents into pan.
  7. Cook for five minutes.
  8. Flip pancake over.
  9. Lather some more vegan butter over the top.
  10. Cooke for four more minutes.
  11. Remove pancake for pan by placing it on a plate.
  12. Turn off burner.
  13. Remove pan from burner.
  14. Serve pancake.
